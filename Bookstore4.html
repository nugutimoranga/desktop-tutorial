About Us:

Nestled in the heart of downtown, The Reading Nook has been Shankling's coziest bookstore since 1987.

We pride ourselves on creating a warm, quiet space for book lovers of all ages to explore, discover, and connect.

Our café offers locally roasted coffee and cozy reading nooks to settle in with your favorite title.
